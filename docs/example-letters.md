# Example letters for testing

Paste any of these into the app to test it (especially once a real API key is set).
All are fictional samples.

## 1. DMV — vehicle registration renewal

```
FINAL NOTICE — Vehicle Registration Renewal. Our records show that the registration
for your vehicle (plate 7ABC123) expires on 03/15/2026. To avoid penalties, you must
renew before this date, either online at dmv.ca.gov or by mail using the enclosed form.
A late fee of $25 will be added after the due date, and driving with an expired
registration may result in a citation.
```

## 2. IRS — request for information

```
Internal Revenue Service — Notice CP2000. We received information from third parties
(employers, banks) that does not match the income you reported on your 2024 federal tax
return. This notice proposes changes and shows a proposed amount due of $842. If you
agree, sign and return the enclosed response form by April 30, 2026. If you disagree,
send a signed statement and supporting documents by the same date. This is not a bill.
```

## 3. Landlord — lease renewal and rent increase

```
NOTICE OF LEASE RENEWAL. Your current lease for Unit 4B ends on 05/31/2026. We are
offering a renewal for a 12-month term at a new monthly rent of $2,150 (an increase of
$120). To accept, sign and return this notice by 04/15/2026. If we do not receive your
signed renewal by that date, your tenancy will convert to month-to-month at the new rate,
and either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.
```

## 4. Health insurance — coverage decision

```
Explanation of Benefits — THIS IS NOT A BILL. Claim #55182 for services on 02/10/2026.
Amount billed: $1,240. Plan discount: $610. Plan paid: $504. Your responsibility: $126
(deductible). If you believe this decision is incorrect, you may request an appeal within
180 days by calling the number on your member ID card.
```
